How Inch Calc Works

Basics, results, and tips in one place.

Basics

  • Operators + − × ÷ behave like a tape calculator; C clears everything.
  • Easy fractions: tap 1/16 through 15/16 for common inch fractions.
  • Feet (Ft): type a whole or decimal number then tap Ft; hold Ft for ~1s to preview feet without changing the math.
  • Copy: tap a result to copy it.
  • Repeat (↻): repeats the last operator + term whenever it’s enabled.
  • Undo (hold ←): removes the last “number + its operator” pair.

Results — 3 views

  • Tape view: the red line marks the exact decimal position and the tape auto-fits around your result.
  • Left result: a mixed fraction rounded to the nearest 1/16″.
  • Right result: the precise decimal to 0.0000.

When feet appear anywhere in the expression, inch-only numbers in history get a ″ mark for clarity.

Updates & tips

  • History now shows exact decimals as typed without forcing mixed fractions.
  • Mixed numbers form when you type a whole number and then tap a fraction (e.g., 1 + 1/2 → 1 1/2).
  • Feet preview (hold Ft) shows alternate units without altering the underlying value.
